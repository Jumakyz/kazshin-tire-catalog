FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common customer questions about tire selection and online requests.

How can I choose the correct tire size?

You can check the tire size in your vehicle manual or on the sidewall of your current tire. Example: 205/55 R16.

Do you sell winter tires?

Yes, KazShin offers summer, winter and all-season tires from different brands.

Can I submit a request online?

Yes, customers can use the request form to send their name, phone number and required tire size.

How fast will a manager contact me?

After the request is submitted, a manager can review the information and contact the customer to confirm details.
